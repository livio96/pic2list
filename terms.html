LazyListings provides an AI-powered platform that streamlines the process of listing products for sale on eBay. The core functionality of the Service includes:

  • Photo-Based Product Identification: You upload photos of products you wish to sell. Our AI analyzes these images to identify the product, brand, model, and relevant attributes.

3. eBay Account Connection via OAuth

To use the full functionality of LazyListings, you will be asked to connect your eBay seller account to our Service. This connection is established through eBay's official OAuth 2.0 authorization protocol, which is the industry-standard method for secure, delegated access.

3.1 How OAuth Works

When you choose to connect your eBay account, you will be redirected to eBay's official authorization page. On that page, eBay will ask you to sign in (if not already signed in) and to grant LazyListings specific permissions. You are authorizing eBay to share certain access capabilities with LazyListings. At no point does LazyListings see, handle, or store your eBay username or password.

3.2 What We Request Access To

When you authorize LazyListings through eBay's OAuth system, you grant us the following specific permissions (known as "scopes"):

  • Sell Inventory Access: The ability to create, modify, and manage product listings on your eBay account. This is the core permission that allows us to publish listings on your behalf.
  • Sell Account Access: The ability to read your seller business policies (shipping policies, return policies, and payment policies) so that we can correctly apply them to listings we create for you.
  • Sell Fulfillment Access: The ability to view order and fulfillment information related to listings created through our Service.
  • Commerce Identity (Read-Only): The ability to read your basic eBay profile information (such as your eBay username) for display purposes within the LazyListings dashboard.

3.3 Revoking Access

You may disconnect your eBay account from LazyListings at any time through the Settings page within the application. You may also revoke access directly from your eBay account settings. Upon disconnection, LazyListings will immediately delete all stored OAuth tokens associated with your account, and we will no longer be able to interact with your eBay account in any way.
